Simple Home Systems (SHS)

SHSs are inverter systems designed to power simple loads which includes appliances such as TVs, ACs, coolers, fridges, PCs etc.

They operate using inverters with capacity ranging from 1.5KVA to 5KVA. This inverter has several built-in settings that allow the users to manually select the load hence resulting in optimum power delivery. It has an advanced battery protection system that increases the life and health of the battery.

Tips for using a SHS
  • Make sure you are not running inverters more than the requirements.
  • Inverters can run for hours depending on the size of batteries and power demands.
  • Change your inverter batteries when you feel that they have worn out.
